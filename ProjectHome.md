**pdf2djvu** creates [DjVu](http://djvu.org/) files from PDF files.

It's able to extract:
  * graphics,
  * text layer,
  * hyperlinks,
  * document outline (bookmarks),
  * metadata (including [XMP](http://www.adobe.com/products/xmp/) metadata).
